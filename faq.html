Frequently Asked Questions

I Have A Question

you may want to know

Do I need to make an appointment for your repair services?

We encourage customers to schedule an appointment for our repair services so that we can allocate time for you and expedite the repair process. However, we also accommodate walk-in repairs whenever possible, to cater to urgent needs.

How are repair costs determined?

Repair costs depend on the specific issue with the phone and the required repair work. Our technicians assess the phone and provide you with a detailed breakdown of the repair costs. We strive to offer high-quality repair services at reasonable prices.

Do you provide warranties?

Yes, we provide a limited repair warranty. If you experience any issues related to the repair within the warranty period, please contact us, and we will assist you in resolving the problem.

How long does it take to repair my phone?

Repair time depends on the specific issue and the phone model. Most common issues can be repaired within the same day, but more complex problems may take longer. We strive to repair your phone as quickly as possible and keep you informed about the progress.

If you can't fix my phone, will I get a refund?

In rare cases where we are unable to repair a phone due to severe damage or other issues, we will refund the repair cost you have paid. We always aim to provide satisfactory repair outcomes for our customers.

My phone got water damaged. Can you fix it?

Yes, we offer water damage repair services for phones. Please bring your water-damaged phone to our repair center as soon as possible, and our technicians will conduct a thorough inspection and repair.
